Flaeskestej

Time: 90min

    Ingredients:
  • Boneless pork roast with rind (about 1-1.2 kg)
  • Salt, coarse
  • 3 to 6 dried Bay leaves

    • Instructions:

    Use a sharp knife to cut long deep grooves in the rind of the pork roast. The grooves should be about 5 mm apart. Rub the entire roast thoroughly with coarse salt. Place 3-6 dried Bay leaves in the grooves. Place the roast on a rack with a roasting pan underneath. In the roasting pan; add 2 cups of water and some cut carrots and onions. Preheat the oven to 225 C and cook the roast for 15 minutes. Turn down the heat to 200 C and continue cooking. When the core temperature is 57 C turn on the grill function in the oven. This make sure that the rind gets nice and crisp. The roast is done when the core temperature is 65 C. When the roast is done; take it out of the oven. Let it rest for about 15 minutes or eat it right away. Cut the roast in slices at each groove. If you want to make a nice gravy; drain the vegetables form the water and pour it in a sauce pan. Add some heavy cream and some brown gravy coloring. Add salt to taste.
